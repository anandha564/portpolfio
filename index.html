"header header": The header spans two columns. "sidebar content": The sidebar and content are side by side. "footer footer": The footer spans two columns. Fixed and Flexible Grid Areas in Practice Fixed Areas: The header, footer, and sidebar have fixed dimensions (100px, 50px, and 200px respectively). Flexible Areas: The content area (1fr) will expand to fill the available space. By using this combination of fixed (px) and flexible (fr) units, you can create a layout where specific grid areas have fixed sizes while others adjust to fit the remaining space.
